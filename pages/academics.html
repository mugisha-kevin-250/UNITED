Academics

Our curriculum and educational programs

Ages 3-5

Nursery Education

Our nursery program provides a nurturing environment where young children begin their educational journey. We focus on developing motor skills, social interaction, and early literacy.

  • Play-based learning
  • Motor skill development
  • Social skills building
  • Early math & literacy
Ages 6-12

Primary Education

Our primary program follows the Rwandan national curriculum, preparing students for secondary education while developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

  • Rwandan curriculum
  • English medium instruction
  • Science & math focus
  • Character development
What We Teach

Core Subjects

Mathematics

From basic counting to advanced problem-solving, we build strong mathematical foundations.

Science

Hands-on experiments and exploration of the natural world.

Language Arts

Reading, writing, speaking, and comprehension skills in English and Kinyarwanda.

Special Program

Nutrition Program

We provide daily meals to all students as part of our commitment to holistic education. A well-fed child is better able to learn and thrive.

  • Balanced breakfast and lunch
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Clean drinking water
  • Health monitoring
