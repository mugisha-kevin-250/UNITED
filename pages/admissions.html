About Our School

Building Brighter Futures

United Nursery and Primary School is a leading educational institution located in Musanze, Rwanda. Since our founding, we have been committed to providing world-class education that combines academic rigor with character development.

  • State-of-the-Art Facilities

Admission Requirements

Nursery (Play Group - P3)

  • 2 Passport Photos

Primary (P1 - P6)

Ages 6-12 years

  • Previous School Reports
  • Transfer Certificate

  • Proof of Residence

  • Emergency Contact Info
  • Passport-size Photos
