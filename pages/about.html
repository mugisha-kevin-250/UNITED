About Us

Building Bright Futures in Musanze

Our Mission

To provide quality education and nutrition to children from disadvantaged families in Rwanda, ensuring every child has the opportunity to reach their full potential and contribute to the development of our nation.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Rwanda that transforms the lives of children through holistic education, nutrition, and community support, creating a brighter future for all.

Our Story

History of United School

Founded over 15 years ago, United Nursery and Primary School began as a small community initiative to address the needs of orphaned and vulnerable children in the Musanze district. What started with just 20 students has grown into a thriving institution serving over 500 children.

Our unique approach combines academic excellence with a comprehensive nutrition program, ensuring that students are not only educated but also well-fed and healthy. We believe that a hungry child cannot learn, and a well-nourished child can achieve anything.

Today, we continue to expand our programs and reach more children in need, thanks to the support of our community partners and well-wishers around the world.
